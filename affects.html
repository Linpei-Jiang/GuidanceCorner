Physical Effects of Alcohol

  • Slower reaction time — affects coordination and balance
  • Dehydration + fatigue — headaches, dizziness
  • Stomach irritation — nausea, vomiting
  • Heart strain — increased heart rate and blood pressure

Mental Health Effects

  • Impaired judgment — risky decisions
  • Anxiety + depression — worsens after drinking
  • Memory + focus issues
  • Sleep disruption

Effects on Teens

  • Learning + memory impact
  • Higher addiction risk
  • Emotional development disruption
  • Accidents + injuries

Long‑Term Damage

  • Liver disease
  • Heart problems
  • Cancer risks
  • Permanent brain changes
