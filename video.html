National Hotlines & Support

  • SAMHSA National Helpline

    24/7 free, confidential help for substance use and mental health.

    https://www.samhsa.gov/find-help/helplines

  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

    Call or text 988 for emotional crises, substance-related distress, or safety concerns.

    https://988lifeline.org

  • Partnership to End Addiction

    Support and resources for teens, parents, and families dealing with alcohol misuse.

    https://drugfree.org

  • Alcoholics Anonymous (AA)

    Meetings and recovery support for people who want to stop drinking.

    https://www.aa.org

Educational Alcohol-Awareness Resources

  • NIAAA – National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism

    Science-based information on alcohol’s effects on the brain, body, and development.

    https://www.niaaa.nih.gov

  • CDC – Alcohol & Public Health

    Statistics, health risks, and prevention strategies related to alcohol use.

    https://www.cdc.gov/alcohol

  • NIDA for Teens – Alcohol

    Teen-friendly explanations of how alcohol affects the brain and behavior.

    https://teens.drugabuse.gov/drug-facts/alcohol

  • StopAlcoholAbuse.gov

    Federal resources, prevention campaigns, and youth-focused materials.

    https://www.stopalcoholabuse.gov

    https://www.thetrevorproject.org
